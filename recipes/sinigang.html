Pork Sinigang

Description

Chicken Adobo is a traditional Filipino dish. It's very easy to cook, and all the ingredients are readily available in any stores.

This is perfect for lunch or as a comfort food. Serve with a steaming cup of white rice.

Ingredients

  • 1 kilo bone-in chicken thigh
  • 1/2 cup soy sauce
  • 1/2 cup vinegar
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 3 cloves garlic, peeled(you can add more if you want)
  • 2 large onions halved and chopped
  • ground pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Put a pan on the stove, set it to medium high.
  2. Put the chicken and the garlic in the pan. Oil is not necessary.
  3. Put a lid on the pan and wait for the fat to render.
  4. Flip the chicken thighs it turns translucent.
  5. When the fat is rendered, put in the water, vinegar, and soy sauce.
  6. Bring it to a boil to cook the chicken thoroughly and reduce the sauce. Boil it for 5 to 10 minutes and flip the chicken every 3 minutes or so to cook it evenly.
  7. After 10minutes, lower the heat to bring it to a simmer. Add in the onions and cover it with a lid
  8. Reduce the sauce further according to your liking, this will help intensify the flavors. Stir from time to time to avoid burning the soy sauce.
  9. When simmering is almost done, add in some ground pepper to taste and put in the remaining onion.
  10. When the sauce consistency is to your preference, turn off the heat. Serve the chicken adobo while hot.
